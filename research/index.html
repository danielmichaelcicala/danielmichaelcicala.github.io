--- Research ---

I am an applied category theorist working in network theory. This is an interdisciplinary field, with practitioners coming from pure and applied mathematics, computer science, the natural sciences, and the social sciences. There are both theoretical and applied arms to this field and I strive to strike a balance between the two.
